**Privacy Policy for Sunset****** *Last updated: June 7, 2026*** **The short version:** Sunset collects no data. Nothing leaves your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no servers. **What data does the app use?****** Sunset asks for your birth year, country, and a projected end-of-life year. This information is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults and is shared only between the app and its widget extension via a private app group container. This data is never transmitted off your device. **Data collection****** Sunset does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. There is no backend. There are no network requests. There is no telemetry or crash reporting. **Third-party services****** Sunset uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or external services of any kind. **Tracking****** Sunset does not track you. The app does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track. **Data deletion****** Since all data is stored locally on your device, deleting the app removes all associated data. There is nothing to request deletion of because nothing exists elsewhere. **Children's privacy****** Sunset does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. **Changes to this policy****** If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. **Contact****** If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at pihumventures@gmail.com GitHub Copilot:
